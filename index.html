Rambu-What?

Despite it’s funny hairy red exterior, Rambutan is actually white and soft on the inside, and somewhat resembles a combination of a kiwi and a lychee. And not only is it real, but it also has some amazing health benefits that make it even more cooler than it looks.

Get energized

Rambutan is a great source of carbohydrates, proteins and fiber.

Get hydrated

Rambutan contains a high water content, and lowers your thirst levels.

Get healthier

Rambutan is high in Vitamin C, which supports the body’s immune system.
